## EARLY COLD WAR (1945–1960)

- **Country:** Iran
- **Description:** Overthrow of PM Mohammad Mosaddegh; restored Shah to power

### Operation PBSUCCESS — Guatemala (1954)
- **Country:** Guatemala
- **Description:** Removal of President Jacobo Árbenz Guzmán; psychological warfare, propaganda, paramilitary action; first CIA assassination manuals prepared
- **Confirmation:** Declassified 1994 CIA report by Nicholas Cullather; NSC authorization docs; FRUS
- **Category:** Regime Change, Assassination Planning
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — HIGH PRIORITY

### CIA Tibetan Operations (1950–1960)
- **Country:** Tibet
- **Description:** Support for Tibetan resistance to Chinese Communist rule; paramilitary training
- **Confirmation:** Declassified CIA documents; Digital National Security Archive

- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet

### U-2 Spy Program (1956–1962)
- **Country:** Soviet Union, China, Vietnam, Cuba
- **Description:** High-altitude photographic reconnaissance; 250+ overflights; Francis Gary Powers incident (May 1, 1960)

## VIETNAM ERA (1960–1973)

### Operation MONGOOSE — Cuba (1961–1962)
- **Country:** Cuba
- **Description:** Covert campaign post-Bay of Pigs; sabotage, terrorism, assassination attempts on Castro; led by William King Harvey and Edward Lansdale
- **Confirmation:** 2001 declassification of 400 pages; National Security Archive; JFK Executive Order Nov 30, 1961
- **Category:** Assassination, Sabotage, Covert Action
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — HIGH PRIORITY

### Bay of Pigs / Operation ZAPATA (1961)
- **Country:** Cuba
- **Description:** Paramilitary invasion to overthrow Castro; catastrophic failure
- **Confirmation:** CIA IG Kirkpatrick's 1961 internal report (FOIA 1998); Taylor Commission; NSA declassification
- **Category:** Paramilitary, Regime Change
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — HIGH PRIORITY

### Operation PHOENIX — Vietnam (1968–1972)
- **Country:** Vietnam
- **Description:** Program to destroy Viet Cong political infrastructure; infiltration, assassination, torture; 20,000+ killed
- **Confirmation:** Declassified CIA docs; Congressional hearings; State Dept FRUS; CIA reading room
- **Category:** Counterinsurgency, Assassination, Torture
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet

### CIA Laos Secret War (1960–1975)
- **Country:** Laos
- **Description:** Largest CIA paramilitary op to date; 30,000+ local fighters (mainly Hmong) under General Vang Pao; secret base at Long Cheng; 400+ daily flights
- **Confirmation:** Declassified CIA docs; Congressional hearings 1971; CIA official history; 2008 declassifications
- **Category:** Paramilitary Operations, Covert War
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — HIGH PRIORITY

### Operation CHAOS — Domestic Surveillance (1967–1974)
- **Country:** United States
- **Description:** Domestic espionage; 7,200 files on Americans; 300,000 civilians indexed; infiltration of universities; anti-war and civil rights targeting
- **Confirmation:** Rockefeller Commission (1975); Church Committee; admitted by DCI George H.W. Bush
- **Category:** Domestic Surveillance, Illegal Operations
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet

### CIA Involvement in Watergate (1972–1974)
- **Country:** United States
- **Description:** CIA operatives in White House Plumbers; 5/5 Watergate burglars were ex-CIA; Helms withheld info from FBI; internal CIA Watergate history declassified 2016
- **Confirmation:** CIA "Working Draft: CIA Watergate History" (155 pages, 2016); Congressional investigations

## COLD WAR CONTINUATION (1970s–1980s)

- **Country:** Chile
- **Description:** Prevented Allende's election (1970); destabilised his government; funded El Mercurio; led to 1973 coup
- **Confirmation:** Church Committee (Dec 4, 2024 release); SSCI "Covert Action in Chile 1963–1973"; Nixon/Kissinger directed ops Sep 15, 1970
- **Category:** Regime Change, Political Covert Action
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — HIGH PRIORITY

### Operation CONDOR — South American Network (1975–1983)
- **Country:** Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Paraguay, Uruguay, Bolivia
- **Description:** CIA provided computers for centralised databank; secret comms system (Condortel); backed cross-border assassination/disappearance campaigns against leftists
- **Confirmation:** Declassified CIA June 1976 report; National Security Archive; Kissinger intelligence briefing Aug 1976
- **Category:** International Covert Action, Political Repression
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet

### CIA Brazil 1964 Coup Support
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Description:** Covert support for military coup against President Goulart; Operation Brother Sam; CIA funding of street demos; carrier task force deployed offshore
- **Confirmation:** Declassified audiotape and docs (40th anniversary release); State Dept FRUS; Ambassador Gordon telegrams
- **Category:** Regime Change, Covert Action
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet

### Operation GLADIO — NATO Stay-Behind Armies (1945–1990)
- **Country:** Italy, Belgium, France, Germany, Spain, Greece, Turkey + 8 more
- **Description:** Clandestine armed resistance networks across 15 European countries; linked to right-wing terrorism; exposed by Italian PM Andreotti Nov 1990
- **Confirmation:** Italian Senate 1990; European Parliament debate Nov 22, 1990; Judge Guido Salvini findings; declassified NATO documents
- **Category:** Paramilitary, Covert Action, False Flag
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — HIGH PRIORITY

### Operation CYCLONE — Afghanistan (1979–1992)
- **Country:** Afghanistan
- **Description:** Arm and finance Afghan mujahideen against Soviets; $20–30M/year (1980) rising to $630M (1987); total >$20B; routed through Pakistan
- **Confirmation:** Presidential Finding July 3, 1979; declassified FRUS; Guinness World Records — most expensive covert action
- **Category:** Paramilitary, Foreign Covert Action
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — HIGH PRIORITY

### Iran-Contra / CIA Nicaragua Operations (1981–1987)
- **Country:** Nicaragua
- **Description:** CIA support for Contras against Sandinistas; port mining (1984); violated Boland Amendment; Oliver North; drug trafficking connections
- **Confirmation:** Iran-Contra Committee investigations; declassified North docs; National Security Archive; Congressional report
