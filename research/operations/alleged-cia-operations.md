## HIGH PROFILE

### CIA-Contra Crack Cocaine Trafficking (1980s–1990s)
- **Years:** 1981–1990
- **Country:** Nicaragua, United States (Los Angeles)
- **Description:** CIA-backed Contra forces financed operations through cocaine trafficking into LA, fuelling the crack epidemic in Black communities. Investigated by Gary Webb's 1996 *Dark Alliance* series (San Jose Mercury News).
- **Why Alleged Not Confirmed:** Three federal investigations found no CIA conspiracy. However, the CIA's own 1998 IG report acknowledged 50+ Contra units were involved in drug trafficking, the CIA knew in real time, and the agency covered it up for over a decade.
- **Evidence level:** CIA cover-up confirmed; active CIA orchestration of drug imports — not confirmed.
- **Primary source:** Gary Webb (San Jose Mercury News, 1996); CIA Inspector General Report 1998
- **Category:** Drug Trafficking, Organised Crime
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — HIGH PROFILE
- **Notes:** Gary Webb was found dead in 2004, ruled suicide (two gunshot wounds to the head — widely disputed). Could be its own article.

### Operation MOCKINGBIRD — CIA Media Infiltration (1950s–ongoing alleged)
- **Years:** ~1950s–1970s (alleged ongoing)
- **Country:** United States
- **Description:** Alleged large-scale CIA program to recruit and control US journalists and news organisations for propaganda. Deborah Davis claimed 400+ US press members recruited including NYT and Time figures.
- **Why Alleged Not Confirmed:** Church Committee (1975) confirmed CIA-journalist relationships and CIA connections to 400 journalists. Carl Bernstein's 1977 Rolling Stone investigation documented this. But no evidence of "Operation Mockingbird" as a single coordinated program with that name.
- **Evidence level:** CIA-journalist connections confirmed; systematic named program — not confirmed.
- **Primary source:** Deborah Davis; Carl Bernstein (Rolling Stone, 1977); Church Committee 1975
- **Category:** Media Manipulation, Domestic Propaganda
- **Archive status:** ✅ Article exists (`operation-mockingbird.mdx`) — article could be stronger on the distinction between what's confirmed and what's alleged

### JFK Assassination — CIA Involvement (1963)
- **Years:** 1963 (documents released to 2025)
- **Country:** United States
- **Description:** Longstanding allegations of CIA involvement in JFK's assassination. CIA had confirmed anti-Kennedy operations (Bay of Pigs humiliation, assassination plots it ran, surveillance of Oswald).
- **Why Alleged Not Confirmed:** March 2025 release of 77,000 pages found no evidence of CIA involvement in the assassination. Warren Commission lone-gunman conclusion stands. Circumstantial motive exists but no documentary trail linking CIA to the shooting.
- **Evidence level:** Motive and opportunity (circumstantial); documentary evidence — not found.
- **Primary source:** National Security Archive 2025; Warren Commission; Church Committee; RFK Jr. statements
- **Category:** Political Assassination
- **Archive status:** ⚠️ Partial article exists (`jfk-cia-theory.mdx`) — should be updated with 2025 releases
- **Notes:** The 2025 JFK document release is a live SEO event. Worth refreshing the article immediately.

## MEDIUM-HIGH PROFILE

### 9/11 CIA Foreknowledge (1999–2001)
- **Years:** 1999–2001
- **Country:** United States
- **Description:** CIA and FBI identified hijackers Khalid al-Mihdhar and Nawaf al-Hazmi as al-Qaeda operatives in 1999 but withheld information from FBI and State Department. LIHOP (Let It Happen On Purpose) allegations.
- **Why Alleged Not Confirmed:** 9/11 Commission confirmed intelligence failures and inter-agency information withholding. CIA IG concluded "good operational follow-through might have resulted in surveillance." Intentional sabotage vs. institutional dysfunction — not established.
- **Evidence level:** Documented failure + withholding confirmed; deliberate intent — not confirmed.
- **Primary source:** 9/11 Commission Report; CIA Inspector General; declassified NSA/CIA communications
- **Category:** Intelligence Failure / Alleged Deliberate Sabotage
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — HIGH INTEREST

### CIA Electoral Interference — Global (1946–2000)
- **Years:** 1946–2000
- **Country:** Italy (1948, 1983 confirmed), Peru, Paraguay, and 80+ others alleged
- **Description:** CIA allegedly interfered in foreign elections. Researcher Dov Levin documented CIA intervention in 81 of 938 national elections 1946–2000. Many remain classified.
- **Why Alleged Not Confirmed:** Some cases confirmed (Italy 1948); many recent cases lack declassification.
- **Evidence level:** Italy confirmed; dozens of others alleged pending declassification.
- **Primary source:** Dov Levin study; Foreign Affairs; declassified State Dept cables
- **Category:** Electoral Interference, Covert Political Operations

## MEDIUM PROFILE

### MLK Assassination — CIA/FBI Involvement (1968)
- **Years:** 1968
- **Country:** United States
- **Description:** Allegations of FBI/CIA involvement in MLK's assassination, based on confirmed COINTELPRO targeting and known FBI animus toward King ("the most dangerous Negro in America" per Hoover memo).
- **Why Alleged Not Confirmed:** House Select Committee on Assassinations (1979) found no federal/state/local government involvement. DOJ 2000 found allegations "not substantiated." Confirmed: FBI surveilled, harassed, and sent King a letter urging him to suicide. Assassination itself — no confirmed link.
- **Evidence level:** FBI targeting and harassment confirmed; assassination involvement — not substantiated.
- **Primary source:** HSCA; DOJ investigation; COINTELPRO files; DNI 2025 release
- **Category:** Political Assassination
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet

### RFK Assassination — CIA/Hypnosis (Sirhan Sirhan) (1968)
- **Years:** 1968
- **Country:** United States
- **Description:** Allegations that Sirhan Sirhan was CIA-hypnotised as a programmed assassin. Sirhan's lawyers alleged CIA involvement in 2010. Filmmaker alleged CIA operatives present at scene.

### CIA-BCCI Money Laundering (1980s–1992)
- **Years:** 1980s–1992
- **Country:** Global (Pakistan, Middle East, Latin America)
- **Description:** Allegations that CIA used Bank of Credit and Commerce International (BCCI) to launder drug trafficking proceeds and finance covert operations including Contra funding. William Casey's alleged relationship with BCCI entities.
- **Why Alleged Not Confirmed:** No confirmed direct CIA-BCCI orchestrated money laundering. Related: Contra groups moved funds through narcotics/money laundering (confirmed); BCCI handled CIA operational accounts (confirmed); full conspiracy — not proven.
- **Evidence level:** CIA used BCCI accounts (confirmed); deliberate laundering scheme — not confirmed.
- **Primary source:** Congressional investigations; IRP/FAS Congressional report; journalist inquiries
- **Category:** Financial Crimes, Organised Crime
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — medium priority

### Gary Webb's Death — Murder Allegation (2004)
- **Years:** 2004
- **Country:** United States
- **Description:** Journalist Gary Webb, who broke the CIA-Contra cocaine story, was found dead in 2004 with two gunshot wounds to the head. Ruled suicide by Sacramento coroner. Widely alleged to be CIA retaliation.
- **Why Alleged Not Confirmed:** Medical examiner ruled suicide. Two shots to the head from a self-inflicted gunshot is rare but documented. No direct evidence of CIA involvement. Webb had been professionally destroyed and was depressed. Suicide note reportedly existed.
- **Evidence level:** Circumstances suspicious; CIA involvement — no evidence.
- **Primary source:** Sacramento Bee; *Kill the Messenger* (film/book); Webb family
- **Category:** Alleged Murder, Whistleblower Retaliation
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — could be a sidebar in the Contra cocaine article

- **Primary source:** Declassified State Dept cables; Shane & Risen (NYT 2003); National Security Archive
- **Category:** Regime Support, War Crimes Complicity

### CIA & Unit 8200 — Stuxnet (2009–2010)
- **Years:** 2009–2010
- **Country:** Iran
- **Description:** Cyberweapon targeting Iranian nuclear centrifuges at Natanz facility. Widely attributed to NSA/CIA and Israel's Unit 8200 under codename Olympic Games. Obama administration never officially confirmed.
- **Why Alleged Not Confirmed:** NYT and other outlets reported Obama personally authorised; Snowden confirmed US involvement. No official US government acknowledgement.
- **Evidence level:** Snowden confirmation; journalistic sourcing strong; official US confirmation — absent.
- **Primary source:** David Sanger, *Confront and Conceal* (NYT); Snowden documents
- **Category:** Cyberwarfare, Covert Action
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — HIGH INTEREST (modern, tech angle)

### CIA Drug Trafficking — Golden Triangle (Vietnam Era)
- **Years:** 1960s–1970s
- **Country:** Laos, Thailand, South Vietnam
- **Description:** Allegations that CIA's Air America (confirmed front airline) transported opium from Hmong villages in the Golden Triangle to fund covert Laos operations.
- **Why Alleged Not Confirmed:** Air America confirmed CIA front. Alfred McCoy's *The Politics of Heroin in Southeast Asia* (1972) documented the allegation extensively. CIA pressured Yale to prevent McCoy's research. Active CIA facilitation of drug trafficking — never officially confirmed.
- **Evidence level:** Air America confirmed; drug transport allegations documented by multiple researchers; CIA facilitation — not officially confirmed.
- **Primary source:** Alfred McCoy; Air America veterans; declassified flight records
- **Category:** Drug Trafficking, Covert Operations
- **Archive status:** 🔴 No article yet — could fold into Laos Secret War article
